My professional life ...

I could have taken this opportunity to retire. For all these years, my hand on experience was limited to telecom software development. Falling behind the new internet technology. I decide to go back to school catch up. When I told my kids my decision, they were so happy. They thought they could track my grades on quiz, mid-term and final, like what I did to them. Thanks for Professor Stolley! We don’t do any test. My kids are disappointed!
